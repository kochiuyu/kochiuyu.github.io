PhD Conference

I am the coordinator the Second Singapore Economics PhD Conference (2nd SEPC) will be held at the National University of Singapore on 18th November, 2016 (Friday). The first conference (1st SEPC) was organized by the Division of Economics at Nanyang Technological University on 22nd April, 2016. This conference series aims to feature the collective works of Economics PhD students based in Singapore.

For details, please visit https://sites.google.com/site/sgeconphdcon/
